What are promo codes and how to use them

At Icarus Digital Marketing, we’ve designed our Promo System to make requesting a quote for our exclusive offers as simple and seamless as possible.

Here’s everything you need to know to get started

Explore Our Promos

Browse through our list of exclusive promotional packages available on this page. Each promo is labeled with a unique Promo Code for easy identification.

Note the Promo Code

When you find a promo that suits your needs, make a note of its unique code (e.g., Promo #001). This code helps us quickly identify the specific package you’re interested in.

Submit the Contact Form

Head over to our Contact/Request a Quote Form and fill in your details. Be sure to include the Promo Code in the designated field. This ensures we know exactly which package you’re inquiring about.

Receive Your Formal Quote

Once we receive your form submission, our team will prepare a detailed, personalized quote based on your selected promo. You’ll receive this via email shortly.
